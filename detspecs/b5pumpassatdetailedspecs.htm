Fluid changes/maintenance intervals

Maintenance intervals: above all else, refer to your owner's manual for more detailed specifications, warnings, and notices

Light bulbs: refer to light bulb replacement reference table

Available colors and touch up paint: refer to OEM paint codes

Engine oil:

Full synthetic oils meeting VW 505.01, 506.01, or 507.00 ONLY, 10,000 miles or 6 months, whichever is sooner.

Capacity w/filter change: 4.0L

See 1000q: oil change for the procedure

Click here for engine oil list w/references


Antifreeze/coolant: 

Lifetime fill (100,000 mile) for g12/g12+ VW OEM fluid or Pentosin coolant, pink or red in color.

WARNING: DO NOT MIX OEM or Pentosin with ANY other color coolant or it will turn brown and sludge. If this occurs, you must flush the entire coolant system.

Green coolant will work but must not be mixed with pink coolant or it will turn brown and engine damage will result.

Mix with distilled water only, tap water will leave mineral deposits

See 1000q: coolant flush for the procedure

Brake fluid:

replace every 2 years, regardless of mileage

DOT 4 fluid only, not compatible with DOT 3 fluid

See 1000q: brake fluid flush to see how to change it

Automatic transmission fluid

Lifetime fluid (100,000 mile) replacement

see 1000q: ATF fluid flush for details

Power steering fluid

40,000 miles replacement

VW# G002000 

To change, suck out the old fluid with a turkey baster and put in new fluid.  Drive the car, repeat.

See 1000q: fluid change for more details.

Manual transmission fluid: N/A for b5 passat tdi because there were no manual transmission

Fuel filter

Drain every 10,000 miles, replace every 20,000 miles

See 1000q: fuel filter change for the procedure

Timing belt

80,000 or 100,000 miles replacement

See 1000q: BHW VW Passat TDI timing belt change for details.

Air intake filter

40,000 miles replacement

Cabin air filter

20,000 miles replacement

Brakes/rotors/rear wheel bearings

Rotate wheels every 10,000 miles
