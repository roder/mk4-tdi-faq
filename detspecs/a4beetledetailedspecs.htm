Fluid changes/maintenance intervals

Light bulbs: refer to light bulb replacement reference table

Available colors and touch up paint: refer to OEM paint codes

Engine oil:

  • Conventional oil: 5000 miles or 6 months, whichever is sooner.
  • Semi-synthetic oils: 7,500 miles or 6 months, whichever is sooner. 
  • Full synthetic oils: 10,000 miles or 6 months, whichever is sooner. 
  • In short, approved oils must meet VW 505.00 standard or above
  • Capacity w/filter change: 4.5L
  • Click here for engine oil list w/references
  • How to change your engine oil on the a4


Antifreeze/coolant: 

  • Lifetime fill (100,000 mile) for g12/g12+ VW OEM fluid or Pentosin coolant, pink or red in color.
  • Green coolant can work but must NOT be mixed with pink coolant or it will turn brown and engine damage will result.
  • Mix with distilled water only, tap water will leave mineral deposits
  • Capacity: 6.0 L
  • How to change your antifreeze/coolant: a4

Brake fluid:

  • replace every 2 years, regardless of mileage or as needed
  • DOT 4 fluid only, not compatible with DOT 3 fluid
  • How to change your brake fluid: a4

Clutch fluid:

  • replace every 2 years, regardless of mileage or as needed
  • DOT 4 fluid only, not compatible with DOT 3 fluid
  • How to change your clutch fluid: a4

Automatic transmission fluid

  • Lifetime fluid (100,000 mile) replacement
  • Pentosin      VW#
  • How to change your automatic transmission fluid (ATF): a4

Power steering fluid

  • 40,000 miles replacement
  • VW# G002000 
  • To change, suck out the old fluid with a turkey baster and put in new fluid.  Drive the car, repeat.

Manual transmission fluid

  • Lifetime fluid (100,000 mile) replacement
  • Use API G50 75W-90 (OEM VW) synthetic gea4 oil or GL4 Mil-L2105
  • Fluid capacity for a4: 2.0L
  • Redline okay
  • How to change your manual transmission fluid: a4

Fuel filter

  • Drain every 10,000 miles, replace every 20,000 miles
  • How to change your fuel filter: a4

Timing belt

  • 40,000 miles replacement for auto transmissions, 60,000 miles replacement for manual transmissions, 80,000 miles for retrofitted extended life timing belt (2002+ models)
  • How to change your timing belt: a4 part 1: removal and prep
  • How to change your timing belt: a4 part 2: installation

Air intake filter

  • 40,000 miles replacement
  • How to change the air intake filter: a4

Cabin air filter

  • 20,000 miles replacement
  • How to change your cabin air filter: a4

Brakes/rotors/rear wheel bearings

  • Rotate wheels every 10,000 miles
  • How to change your brakes: a4 front and rear brakes
