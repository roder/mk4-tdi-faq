Fluid changes/maintenance intervals

Light bulbs: refer to light bulb replacement reference table

Available colors and touch up paint: refer to OEM paint codes

Engine oil:

  • Conventional oil: 5000 miles or 6 months, whichever is sooner.
  • Semi-synthetic oils: 7,500 miles or 6 months, whichever is sooner. 
  • Full synthetic oils: 10,000 miles or 6 months, whichever is sooner. 
  • Capacity w/filter change: 4.5 L 
  • Click here for engine oil list w/references
  • How to change your engine oil on the a3


Antifreeze/coolant: 

  • Lifetime fill (100,000 mile) for g12/g12+ VW OEM fluid or Pentosin coolant, pink or red in color.
  • WARNING: DO NOT MIX OEM or Pentosin with ANY other color coolant or it will turn brown and sludge. If this occurs, you must flush the entire coolant system.
  • Mix with distilled water only, tap water will leave mineral deposits
  • Capacity: 6.5 Liters
  • How to change your antifreeze/coolant: a3

Brake fluid:

  • DOT 4 fluid, not compatible with only DOT 3 spec fluid or silicone based DOT 5
  • Every 2 years or as needed
  • How to change your brake fluid: a3

Clutch fluid

  • DOT 4 fluid, not compatible with DOT 3 fluid
  • How to change your clutch fluid: a3

Power steering fluid

  • 40,000 miles
  • VW# G002000 
  • To change, suck out the old fluid with a turkey baster and put in new fluid.  Drive the car, repeat.

Manual transmission fluid

  • Lifetime fluid (100,000 mile) change interval
  • Use API G50 75W-90 (OEM VW) synthetic gear oil or GL4 Mil-L2105
  • Fluid capacity for a3: 2.1 quarts/2 liters
  • How to change your manual transmission fluid: a3

Fuel filter

  • 20,000 miles replacement
  • How to change your fuel filter: a3
  • http://www.worldimpex.com/item_detail.html?sku=162345

Timing belt

  • 60,000 miles replacement
  • How to change your timing belt: a3 part 1     How to change your timing belt part 2
  • http://www.worldimpex.com/item_detail.html?sku=6394

Air intake filter

  • 40,000 miles or 2 years, whichever is sooner
  • How to change your air intake filter: a3

Cabin air filter

  • N/A on standard car but can be retrofitted, just buy one from a glx jetta, 
  • How to change cabin air filter: a3

Brakes/rotors/rear wheel bearings

  • Front disc/rear drum, change as needed
  • How to change your brakes: a3 jetta front brakes
  • How to change your brakes: a3 jetta rear brakes
