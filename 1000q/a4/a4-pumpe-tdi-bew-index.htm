Volkswagen MK4 VW TDI "how to" index and DIY maintenance for 2004-2005 Jetta and 2004-2006 New Beetle and Golf pumpe duse TDI

This index is for mk4 2004-2005 Jetta and 2004-2006 Golf and New Beetle TDI.  If you have a 2004-2005 Passat TDI, see 1000q: B5 Passat "how to" index.

In 2005.5 the Jetta became a mk5 so see 1000q: mk5 Jetta DIY index if you're looking for the newer body style.   In North America, the mk4 pumpe duse use the "BEW" TDI engine.  2003 and earlier 4th generation cars are not pumpe duse and use the "ALH" TDI engine.  Review the main index page if that's what you have.  Here are some more checks to see if you have a pumpe duse or not 1000q: cosmetic differences on Jetta  and 1000q: injection pump vs. PD vs. common rail. 

Did you just buy this car or are you looking to buy one?  If so, see 1000q: mk4 TDI new car checklist to see a list of common service /problem areas, catch-up maintenance, and VW quirks.
