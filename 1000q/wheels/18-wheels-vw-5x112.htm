OEM 18" VW stock wheels, 5x112 bolt pattern

back to 1000q: VW and Audi wheel gallery index

Here is a parts listing of stock 18" wheel on VW.  The 5x112 bolt pattern was used on mk5 and newer cars and mk4 VW Passat.

Do you have a better picture, own one of the pictures, or see an error?  Please post the correction in the forums or email me at  and I will change it asap for your benefit, thank you!  Note -if you are lowering the suspension or you have 16" wheels now and are putting on 18" wheels, you may need the limiter kit.  Many report no problems with 18" wheels only.
 (quantity 1) VW# 000 071 501 or 000071501a suspension travel limiter kit 

To install the suspension limiter kit: (thanks to user SGI SGI)

Installation on the front axle:
Move the shock absorber protective tube to the top. Remove the circlip on the delimiter, slide the delimiter onto the piston rod and attach the circlip to the delimiter. Place the shock absorber protective tube back in position again.

Installation on the rear axle:
Remove the shock absorber (3 screws), dismantle the top of the shock absorber (remove one screw), remove the protective tube together with the series buffer. Remove the circlip on the delimiter, slide the delimiter onto the piston rod and attach the circlip to the delimiter.

Fit the standard parts in the reverse order.
